About Natalie

From Sports injuries to general muscle soreness, Natalie Smith brings her years of massage therapy experience to help you recapture the health and physical conditioning you deserve.

Specializing in treatment, Natalie will help plan out your sessions and work with you on attaining your health goals by utilizing the latest in massage techniques and methodologies.

Since graduating from Ashmead College in Vancouver Washington and becoming licensed in 2004, Natalie has been very active in the massage therapy community. Her finely honed skills compliment an intense passion to help her clients reach their therapeutic goals on the road to recovery.

In 2007 Natalie took an extensive class in Ashiatsu Oriental Bar Massage to add to her education in Swedish/Deep Tissue massage and Hot Stone therapy. Now, she looks to build upon this success by opening Head and Tail Massage, a center where she can bring the latest massage therapy treatments to an expanding group of clients.

Sports Massage

Sports massage emphasizes prevention and healing of injuries to the muscles and tendons. It is ideal for people experiencing sports injuries, chronic pain, or restricted range of motion.

One hour | $70 - Series of 3 | $180

Hot Stone Massage

Hot stone treatments are exceptional for pain relief, increasing circulation, opening stagnant energy, and giving you a sense of peaceful grounding.

One hour | $90 - Series of 3 | $230

Trigger Point Therapy

Trigger Point therapy tracks referred pain patterns, identifying root tension points and relieving them with compression. Get to the root of the problem.

One hour | $80 - Series of 3 | $210

Swedish Massage

Swedish massage is exceptionally beneficial for increasing the level of oxygen in the blood, decreasing muscle toxins, improving circulation and flexibility while easing tension.

One Hour | $75 - Series of 3 | $190 (add Deep Tissue for $10)

Prenatal Massage

Prenatal massage relieves many of the normal discomforts experienced during pregnancy, such as backaches, stiff neck, leg cramps, headaches and loss of sleep. It is a healthy way to reduce stress and promote overall wellness.

One Hour | $75 - Series of 3 | $190
